My Journey

Hi, I'm Ange. My belief is that we all have beautiful bodies and we need to get out of our heads and into our beautiful bodies to really appreciate our selves. we can have fun in life at the same time.... I love doing this through yoga and meditation, sprinkled with a healthy does of fun, dancing, great food and wine. My hope is that something here will resonate with you and you will be inspired to live your truth. For me it has been a domino affect in my life. The more that I come from authenticity, the more that I am met with that by the people in my life and the new friends I attract.
